AI Governance Policy Pack

Applies To: HyperBuild AI

1. Governance Objectives

  • Maintain human authority
  • Prevent autonomous harm
  • Ensure accountability

2. Policy Domains

  • Security
  • Compliance
  • Operational risk
  • Human oversight

3. Human-in-the-Loop Policy

  • No production execution without approval
  • All high-risk actions reviewed
  • Emergency stop always available

4. Audit & Accountability

  • Every action logged
  • Every rule decision explainable
  • Every approval traceable

5. Legal Position

AI is not a legal actor. Responsibility always remains with the organization.

6. Governance Verdict

HyperBuild AI aligns with emerging AI governance and regulatory expectations when policies are enforced.
